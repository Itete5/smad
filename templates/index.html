Superconducting Materials Automated Discovery

SMAD is a scientific discovery platform for designing, optimizing, and analyzing superconducting materials using high‑performance computing and AI‑driven automation.

Powered by your local SMAD database and HPC workflows.
Key capabilities
  • • Automated structure generation and screening
  • • HPC job orchestration for quantum calculations
  • • AI‑assisted optimization of \(T_c\), stability, and transport
  • • Interactive analysis dashboards for candidate materials

A platform for data‑driven superconductivity

Combine curated materials data, reproducible workflows, and scalable compute to explore new superconducting phases and optimize known families.

Design & generation

Build candidate structures from templates, chemical rules, or AI models and route them directly into high‑throughput DFT or model Hamiltonian calculations.

Optimization at scale

Run multi‑objective optimization campaigns balancing critical temperature, stability, synthesis feasibility, and cost across large chemical spaces.

Analysis & reporting

Inspect structures, band gaps, energy landscapes, and derived observables, and export reproducible summaries for collaborators or publications.

From idea to candidate superconductor

Encode your workflow once and let SMAD coordinate calculations, AI models, and data management across your cluster.

1 · Define space

Select chemistry, structure prototypes, and constraints for exploration.

2 · Launch campaigns

Submit large ensembles of calculations to your HPC scheduler with reproducible inputs.

3 · Learn & optimize

Train surrogate models and use Bayesian or evolutionary search to prioritize new runs.

4 · Analyze & export

Inspect promising candidates, export structures, and share notebooks or reports.
