About SMAD

SMAD (Superconducting Materials Automated Discovery) is a research‑grade platform intended for computational superconductivity projects running on institutional clusters or cloud HPC.

This instance is configured for your environment and can be extended with custom workflows, schedulers, and analysis dashboards tailored to your research group or lab.

Getting started
  1. Populate `smad.db` with materials of interest (e.g. from Materials Project or in‑house data).
  2. Configure SSH access in `config.py` to point to your login node.
  3. Extend the FastAPI backend with routes for launching and tracking calculations.
