Superconducting critical temperature (Tc)

Tc ≈ f(λ, ωlog, μ*)

Where:

  • λ = electron–phonon coupling
  • ωlog = logarithmic phonon frequency
  • μ* = Coulomb pseudopotential
  • DOS(EF) = density of states at Fermi level

Other key quantities

  • Debye temperature — characteristic phonon energy scale
  • Spin–orbit coupling — relevant for heavy elements and topology
  • Symmetry — space group, point group, and representation analysis
  • Thermoelectric figure of merit (ZT) — ZT = S²σT/(κe+κL) for thermoelectric performance
