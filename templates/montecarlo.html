Monte Carlo Simulations

Configure and run Monte Carlo simulations: MCMC, kinetic Monte Carlo (kMC), Parallel Tempering, and Grand Canonical Monte Carlo (GCMC) for sampling, kinetics, and phase behavior.

Markov Chain Monte Carlo (MCMC)

Sample from posterior distributions for parameter fitting and uncertainty quantification. Output: chains, autocorrelation, credible intervals.

Kinetic Monte Carlo (kMC)

Simulate rare events and long-time dynamics: diffusion, surface kinetics, defect evolution.

Parallel Tempering (Replica Exchange)

Run replicas at different temperatures with configuration swaps to escape local minima.

Grand Canonical Monte Carlo (GCMC)

Sample at fixed μ, T, V for adsorption, insertion, and open-system equilibria.
